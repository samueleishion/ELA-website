ELA seeks to encourage under represented UTCS students to get involved in leadership, networking, and mentoring with peers, faculty, and professionals in the area of Computer Science both locally and nationally. We seek to empower students by providing opportunities in which they can become engaged in these activities within UT and the minority community in Texas, as well as through participation in conferences offered by the EL Alliance national organization.
